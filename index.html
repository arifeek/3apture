Our Process

From physical space to digital walk-through.

01

Capture the Space

We capture your space using a 360° camera, recording the full environment from multiple positions. This creates a complete visual record of the area.

02

Build the Walk-Through

The captured images are processed into a single, connected walk-through. Viewpoints are linked together so the space can be explored in a clear and consistent way.

03

View Online

The walk-through can be viewed on desktop, tablet, or mobile. It can be shared with others or embedded on a website without requiring special software.

A straightforward way to record and view a space on-line.

Who We Are

Making 3D space capture more widely accessible.

What We Do

3apture creates digital walk-throughs of physical spaces. They allow people to move through a place virtually and understand its layout and structure.

Why It Works

Seeing a space properly makes decisions easier. Our tours are designed to be clear, intuitive, and accessible on any device, so anyone can understand how a place feels and flows without being there in person.

Explore a space online. Get a better sense of it. Then decide what comes next.
